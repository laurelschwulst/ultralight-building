What is ultralight?

Ultralight is a word that means extremely lightweight.

The prefix “ultra” can mean “very”, or “extremely.” So “ultralight” is shorthand for a lightweight aircraft, for instance. Or some kites are so light they can fly indoors. And "ultralight" also describes a genre of lightweight backpacking equipment which ideally makes your journey less burdensome.

But “ultra” can also mean “beyond” or “on the other side of” or “transcending.” For example, the word “ultraviolet” means “light beyond the visible spectrum.” Unlike humans, some other animals can see ultraviolet light, allowing them to perceive sensory information helping in communicating, mate selection, and foraging for food.
